Welcome to Persian Cat SpeakUp

Persian Cat SpeakUp is an independent whistleblowing service managed by Persian Daddy, giving you the opportunity to 'SpeakUp' on impropriety or misdeeds. We want to ensure Persian Cat continues to be a safe, ethical and honest workplace.

Fraud and misconduct has a negative impact on Persian Cat’ workplace and reputation. By reporting such activity you can help ensure Persian Cat continues to be a safe, ethical and honest place to work.

Most reported cases are uncovered through tips from honest people who may not be sure of all the facts - we do not expect you to know every detail. Reporting what you do know is a sufficient enough first step. However, please note that all reporting should be made in good faith and without malicious intent or for personal gains.

Persian Cat SpeakUp provides two channels to make a report, by website or call.

Your identity is protected and will be kept confidential by Persian Daddy, to the extent feasible and permissible under the law or unless you choose to reveal it. Persian Cat SpeakUp provides an option for you to remain anonymous or disclose your identity.

Once you have provided your information to Persian Cat SpeakUp, a report will be made and forwarded to the representative(s) in Persian Cat who is responsible for addressing fraud and misconduct, for their onward investigation.

Persian Cat SpeakUp may contact you for more information, if required (if you have provided your contact details). Also, if you have any new or additional information, you can make a follow up report, quoting the reference number that was initially provided to you.

Don't turn a ‘blind eye’ - SpeakUp and help continue to make Persian Cat a safe, ethical and honest workplace.

What to Report

If you see any suspicious activity, illegal or unethical behaviour in Persian Cat, ‘SpeakUp’ and report it. Such activity or behaviour could include:

  1. Breaches of Professional Integrity / Unethical Behaviour
  2. Breach of Banking Secrecy
  3. Conflict of Interest
  4. Data Leakage / Information Theft
  5. Unauthorised System Access
  6. Criminal Offences where A Staff is Involved
  7. Regulatory Breach

Examples of suspicious activity:

1. Breaches of Professional Integrity / Unethical Behaviour

  • Where a Persian Cat employee does not act in good faith for Persian Cat or does not behave in a manner that promotes trust.
  • Where a Persian Cat employee abuses his/her position or authority for an unfair advantage or benefit, or does so to the detriment of interests to other parties.

2. Breach of Banking Secrecy

  • Where a Persian Cat employee does not maintain the confidentiality of customer's information by disclosing or publishing information relating to customer accounts' affairs and conduct to another party.

3. Conflict of Interest

  • Where a Persian Cat employee holds any undeclared position in another Persian Cat having dealings with Persian Cat.
  • Where a Persian Cat employee has family members or friends in business relationships with Persian Cat and does not disclose it.
  • Where a Persian Cat employee receives any financial or non-financial gifts or entertainment from a vendor/supplier and recommends to award the contract to the vendor/supplier without performing a proper quotation analysis against other vendors/suppliers.

4. Data Leakage / Information Theft

  • Where a Persian Cat employee involves in transmitting data, electronically or physically from within the organisation to external recipient without authorisation or consent.
  • Where a Persian Cat employee leaks any information about Persian Cat such as budgets, vendor quotation, and confidential information to other unauthorised people.
  • Where a Persian Cat employee transmits Persian Cat data for personal purposes and/or benefits.

5. Unauthorised System Access

  • Where a Persian Cat employee accesses into a Persian Cat computer terminal or data system for non-official purposes and/or without authorisation.

6. Staff Involvement in Criminal activities

  • Where a Persian Cat employee violates the law or involves in the activities that are punishable by law such as Bribery & Corruption, Financial Statement Fraud, Forgery, Cheating, Insider Trading, Theft of Assets, unlawful gambling etc.

7. Regulatory Breach

  • Where a Persian Cat employee's actions violates Persian Cat's operating and licensing obligations to any regulatory authority.

8. Other Breaches

The following are breaches which if reported, may be investigated by other departments and organizations:

  • Human resources-related cases - Workplace concerns, Employee grievances, harassment cases, etc.
  • External Fraud - wrongdoing or fraud committed by non-employees of Persian Cat (e.g. Persian Cat' customers, 3rd party fraudsters, etc).

Frequently Asked Questions

FAQ 1: Why should I SpeakUp on fraud and misconduct?

To help continue to make Persian Cat a safe and honest place to work. Fraud and misconduct does have a negative effect on our workplace and reputation. By reporting fraud and misconduct you can help to provide a safe and honest environment and enhance the effectiveness of Persian Cat.

FAQ 2: Can I remain anonymous?

It's all up to you whether you choose to remain anonymous or not. All whistleblowing reports will be investigated regardless of whether they are anonymous, provided that there is sufficient information. However, by fully disclosing your identity, Persian Cat will be able to contact you to clarify relevant matters or obtain further information that may aid in the investigation.

At times, you may not be able to remain anonymous (even if you choose to do so) as your identity may be apparent from the information given. Regardless of whether you choose to reveal your identity or remain anonymous, we will take all reasonable steps to maintain confidentiality and protect your identity. Every effort will be made not to reveal your identity to the extent feasible and permissible under the law.

FAQ 3: What happens when I make a call?

You will not be asked by Persian Cat SpeakUp to provide your name unless you wish to identify yourself. Your report will be known only by a unique reference number, unless you choose to tell Persian Cat SpeakUp your name.

You will be asked to provide your detailed report to an experienced and independent forensic investigator, who may ask you some additional clarification questions, while taking the details of your report.

FAQ 4: What do I need to tell Persian Cat SpeakUp?

Tell Persian Cat SpeakUp as much information as you can when you SpeakUp. For example:

  • Names of people involved;
  • Names of any witnesses (if any);
  • Your name and email address;
  • Details of evidence you know of;
  • The value of any money or asset involved; and
  • How often this incident occurred.

FAQ 5: What if i suspect something is wrong but I am not sure?

Most cases reported are uncovered through tips from honest people who may not be sure of all the facts. This is perfectly all right. We do not expect you will to know every last detail. Reporting what you do know is enough.
